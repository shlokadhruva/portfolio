About Me

My passion for creation sprouted while pursuing business when I spent days building props from scratch for events and was fueled further towards product design with the way in which it can thoughtfully and practically improve daily lives. With a foundation in industrial design amongst other disciplines, my fundamental values along with my skill set aim to produce intuitive, functional, and user-centric designs using design thinking principles. Not only do I view design as a solution to problems, but as that solution that further enhances the experience.

This stemmed from a personal experience I had while trekking a mountain in India. I was truly inspired by the locals who utilized the most basic items in several ways to meet their requirements and made do with whatever little they had. I sat with them and heard stories of their struggles and how they developed their space, despite facing difficulties. Although, they are content with what they have, I could see how redesigning the space would address their problems. This is what inspired me to dive further into spatial design with the goal to ease daily lives, proving design can be limitless.

Communication, empathy and innovation are at the core of my principles with the urge to explore new possibilities by experimentation for growth. I believe the best designs are brought from a profound understanding of users' needs, combined with the relentless pursuit of hard work and skills. I further believe my commitment to quality is driven by collaboration. Insights with diverse perspectives help in making the design innovative by nature. I proceed every project with a sustainable approach, ensuring we give back to Earth as much as we can.
